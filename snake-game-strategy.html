Mastering SnakeZilla: Pro Strategies

Written by the SnakeZilla Gaming Experts

Snake game dekhne mein simple lagta hai, lekin high scores banana ek art hai. Agar aap Dragon Boss mod ya Speed Ninja mein atak rahe hain, toh yeh strategies aapke kaam aayengi.

1. Wall-Hugging Technique

Sabse basic aur effective strategy hai 'Wall-Hugging'. Koshish karein ki aap hamesha arena ke edges ke sath slither karein. Isse arena ka center khali rehta hai aur aapko move karne ke liye zyada space milti hai jab aapki tail lambi ho jati hai.

2. Don't Chase Food Blindly

Jab snake lamba ho jaye, toh apple ke peeche turant mat bhagein. Pehle apni tail ki position check karein. Kai baar food aisi jagah hota hai jahan jane ke baad nikalna mushkil ho jata hai. Ek lamba rasta lena behtar hai bajaye 'suicide turn' lene ke.

3. Portal Mod Masterclass

Portal Snake mein walls dushman nahi, dost hain. Agar food center mein hai aur aap corner mein hain, toh wall ke andar ghusein aur doosri side se bahar niklein. Yeh shortest path hota hai aur isse speed ka control bana rehta hai.

In tips ko follow karke aap SnakeZilla ke leaderboards par top kar sakte hain. All the best!
