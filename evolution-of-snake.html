The Evolution of the Classic Snake Game

Written by the SnakeZilla Editorial Team • Arcade Enthusiasts

Almost everyone has played it at some point. The premise is simple: control a growing line, collect items, and avoid crashing. But how did the classic Snake game become such a phenomenon? Today, we are exploring the snake game history from its arcade roots to modern browsers.

The Journey from Arcade to Mobile

In 1997, Nokia revolutionized mobile gaming. Over 400 million devices carried the monochrome version of Snake. It was simple, addictive, and perfect for the tech of that time. Today, if you want to play classic snake game on browser, you no longer need old hardware.
