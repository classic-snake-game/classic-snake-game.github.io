The Classic Rules, Upgraded

Your goal is simple: navigate the green serpent around the dark arena to eat the glowing red bugs. Every bug you consume makes your snake grow longer, which makes navigating the tight spaces more difficult. Avoid crashing into the outer walls or your own tail, as a single collision results in an instant Game Over!

Dynamic Level Progression

What sets our Modern Snake game apart is the built-in level progression and High Score saving feature. Every time you score 5 points, your snake will Level Up. With each new level, the base speed of the game increases, testing your reflexes and quick thinking. Your highest score is automatically saved locally, so you can always come back to beat your own record.

Tips for Setting a High Score

  • Use the Grid: Stay aligned with the invisible grid. Unnecessary zigzagging increases the chance of trapping yourself.
  • Plan Ahead: As your tail grows longer, always ensure you leave an "exit route" when diving into a tight corner.
