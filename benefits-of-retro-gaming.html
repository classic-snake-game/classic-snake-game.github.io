Benefits of Retro Arcade Games

Modern high-graphics games ke zamane mein bhi log Snake aur Tetris jaise retro games kyun khelte hain? Iska jawab sirf nostalgia nahi, balki science hai.

1. Stress Reduction

Retro games ka gameplay loop 'predictable' hota hai. Yeh aapke brain ko ek 'flow state' mein le jata hai jahan aapka stress level kam ho jata hai. Kuch der Snake khelna ek meditation ki tarah kaam kar sakta hai.

2. Improving Reflexes

Speed Ninja jaise mods mein millisecond ka reaction time chahiye hota hai. Rozana aise games khelne se aapki hand-eye coordination aur decision-making fast hoti hai.

Toh agli baar jab aap break lein, toh ek modded snake game zaroor kheliye. Yeh sirf fun nahi, aapke dimaag ke liye workout bhi hai.
