Play Toxic Jungle Snake Game Online

Are you ready for a true survival challenge? Welcome to the Toxic Jungle, a thrilling variation of the classic snake game. In this dark and hazardous environment, your reflexes and decision-making skills will be put to the ultimate test. Play this free HTML5 browser game instantly on your mobile or desktop device.

Game Rules and Mechanics

Your goal is to hunt the mice to help your snake survive and evolve. However, the jungle is filled with deadly traps. You must avoid the glowing Purple Skulls at all costs! Eating a poison skull will instantly terminate your session. To make things even more intense, every time you eat a mouse, the purple skulls will teleport to a new random location.

Evolution & Level System

The Toxic Jungle does not forgive easily. Every time you score 5 points, you will Level Up. With each new level, your snake will evolve (changing colors to match the toxic environment), move faster, and a brand new poison skull will spawn on the map. Reaching level 5 means you will have to dodge multiple skulls while hunting for food in a shrinking safe space!
